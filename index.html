About Me

Software Developer

As an Electronics Engineering graduate with experience in photonic research, my career began at the intersection of hardware and software, prototyping IoT devices and machine vision systems. A sudden career shift during the pandemic ignited my passion for deep learning in computer vision, successfully pivoting me into the tech industry.

My journey has taken me from building embedded systems and backend data solutions for AI hardware. I'm passionate about writing clean, efficient code and am constantly exploring new technologies. I am eager to collaborate on innovative and challenging projects, leveraging my full-stack embedded expertise to contribute from concept to deployment.

When I'm not coding, you can find me leading teams as a strategic shot-caller in Mobile Legends, staying active through badminton and futsal, or fueling my growth with self-help and professional development books.
